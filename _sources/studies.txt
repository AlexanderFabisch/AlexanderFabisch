Studies
=======

I have studied computer science at the University of Bremen. I am interested
in artificial intelligence, robotics and machine learning. I was member of the
RoboCup team `B-Human <http://www.b-human.de>`_ from 2009 to 2011. In 2012 I
wrote my diploma thesis about learning in compressed space.
